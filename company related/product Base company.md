## ⚡ Simple Difference (Important for Interviews)

**Product-Based Company:**

* Apna product banati hai (e.g. Google Search, Amazon app)
* High salary 💰
* Strong engineering culture
* More ownership

**Service-Based Company:**

* Client ke liye kaam karti hai
* Less pressure (comparatively)
* Stable but slower growth
